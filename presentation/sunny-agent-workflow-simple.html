How Sunny works

A plain-English walkthrough of what happens when a customer messages us.

Read me first

Sunny is the ElectroSun WhatsApp account manager. He runs around the clock. When any customer messages our business number, Sunny reads it, understands what they're asking, checks our live stock and prices, writes a reply, and sends it back, usually within seconds. If the question needs the human team, Sunny alerts the owner on WhatsApp with the customer's details, conversation summary, and a one-tap direct link. Click any box below to see what it does and a real example of how it plays out.
