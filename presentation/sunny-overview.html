01 The problem

Customers reach out anytime. The team can only be in one chat at a time.

Across Lagos, Abuja, Kano and beyond, the first message a buyer sends is often the only window we have. Three pressures compound at once.

Speed

Replies are slow, leads go cold.

Customers ping at 11pm, on weekends, during installs. The longer they wait, the more likely they ring a competitor instead.

Languages

Five languages on one number.

English, Pidgin, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo. The team cannot always switch context fast enough, and tone matters as much as words.

Signal

Hard to spot serious buyers.

Most chats are general questions. The few buyers ready to invoice are buried in the noise unless someone reads every thread.

02 What Sunny does

A friendly account manager that never goes off shift.

Six things Sunny does, every hour of every day, on the same WhatsApp number ElectroSun customers already know.

Replies to every WhatsApp message instantly.

Speaks the customer's language: English, Pidgin, Hausa, Yoruba, Igbo.

Explains ElectroSun's solar systems, sizing, and timelines.

Spots serious buyers and flags them to the team.

Sends the owner a clean summary every hour.

Stores every chat so nothing is ever lost.

03 How it works

From a customer's first message to a warm reply, in seconds.

Every incoming message follows the same path. Two AI models share the work: one reads, one writes. A human is pulled in only when it actually matters.

1

Customer sends a WhatsApp message

To ElectroSun's official WhatsApp Business number.

2

Meta forwards it to Sunny

The webhook is signature-verified so we know the message is real and untampered.

3

Sunny looks up the contact

New visitor or returning customer? The active conversation thread is loaded.

4

The classifier reads it

A fast Claude model decides: who is this, what do they want, which language, do we need a human?

5A Human path

Polite holding reply, owner is alerted

For specific quotes, complaints, warranty claims, or custom designs. The owner gets the full context immediately.

5B Auto reply

Sunny writes a warm, on-brand answer

A smarter Claude model drafts the reply in the customer's language and ElectroSun's tone.

6

The reply goes back through WhatsApp

Same number, no app to install, no link to click. The customer just gets an answer.

7

Everything is saved

The message, the category, the language, the lead's location, load, and timeline. Nothing falls through the cracks.

04 The brain

Two AI models, one job each.

Big questions need a careful answer. Quick triage needs a quick brain. We split the work so we get both quality and low cost.

Step 4: Classifier

Claude Haiku 4.5

Fast and cheap. Reads each new message in well under a second and produces a strict, structured decision the rest of the system can act on.

  • Picks the customer category (new client, explorer, serious buyer, etc.)
  • Detects the language being used
  • Estimates intent (sizing, pricing, support, complaint)
  • Reports a confidence score
  • Flags whether a human needs to step in
Step 5B: Replier

Claude Sonnet 4.6

The careful, well-spoken one. Reads the full conversation history, knows what we already learned about the customer, and writes the actual reply in Sunny's voice.

  • Stays in the customer's language and register
  • Answers in plain words, never jargon
  • Knows ElectroSun's products, sizing logic, and timelines
  • Asks for the missing fact (location, load) when needed
  • Hands off cleanly when a quote or site visit is required

We use the right size of brain for each job, which keeps quality high and cost low.

05 Categorization

Every customer is sorted into one of six buckets.

The category drives everything that happens next: how Sunny replies, how often we follow up, and whether the team should jump in.

new_client

First message ever

Someone reaching out for the first time, no history yet.

"Hello, I saw your billboard, please can you tell me about your solar systems?"
explorer

General questions, browsing

Curious, gathering information, not yet ready to commit.

"How long does a battery last on average?"
queries_only

Info seeking, not buying

Asking on behalf of someone else, or comparing options for later.

"Do you sell inverters separately, or only the full system?"
serious_buyer

Location, load, timeline

Has the three signals needed to size and quote a real system.

"I'm in Ikeja, I run two ACs, fridge, lights, want to install before end of month."
returning_customer

Mentions a past install or service

Existing customer, often needs maintenance, expansion, or a warranty answer.

"My panel from last year is not charging well in the morning."
spam

Not solar related

Marketing, mistaken number, unrelated requests. Logged but not replied.

"Please send me crypto signals daily."

07 When a human is needed

Sunny knows its limits, and pings the owner the moment it matters.

Most chats are answered in seconds. The few that need a human get a polite holding reply, and the owner gets a clean alert with all the context.

Triggers

  • Customer asks for a specific quote or price.
  • Complaint about an existing installation.
  • Warranty claim on hardware or service.
  • Custom system design request.
  • Sunny is less than 90% confident about its own answer.

10 Built on solid ground

Boring, well-tested pieces. Fewer surprises in production.

Each piece does one thing well. Nothing exotic, nothing unproven, and nothing locked into a single cloud vendor.

Foundation
Node.js 20, the runtime Express, the web server SQLite (better-sqlite3), local storage
Messaging
Meta WhatsApp Cloud API, the official channel
Intelligence
Claude API, by Anthropic Haiku 4.5, classification Sonnet 4.6, replies
Operations
node-cron, schedules the digests Nodemailer, email fallback PM2, autostart and restart
Security
Meta signature verified on every webhook API key on the dashboard endpoints
11 Cost and scale

Light footprint, heavy lifting.

The whole stack runs from the office, not the cloud. The only running cost is the AI calls themselves, which we measure per conversation.

$0.005 to $0.015
Per AI-handled conversation, end to end. Hundreds of conversations a day still cost less than one team coffee run.
24/7
No breaks, no holidays, no missed messages. Replies arrive in seconds, day or night.
1 Mac Mini
Runs on a single machine in the office. No monthly cloud bills, no third party hosting Sunny's brain.
