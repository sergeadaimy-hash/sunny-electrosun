# Solar panels

## What we carry
High wattage mono panels from major brands: Jinko (720W and 620W classes) and Longi (650W class), with JA and Sungrow lines also in the range.
Exact wattages, availability, and prices always come from the Warehouse Stock data; if a wattage the customer names is not in stock, offer the closest stocked wattage instead of confirming the exact one.

## Rules of thumb
Panel count = array size in watts divided by the panel wattage. Example shape: a 5kW array on 650W panels is about 8 panels.
Panels are sized to the inverter and the customer's daytime load plus battery charging; ask the load before recommending a count.

## Common questions and answers
Q: How many panels do I need?
A: Depends on the system size. Share your load or the inverter size and the count follows from the panel wattage.
