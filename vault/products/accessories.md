Q: What extras does a full installation need beyond the inverter, batteries, and panels?
A: Racking or mounting for the batteries and panels, correctly sized cabling, protection (breakers), and for HV builds the matching control box and PDU per cluster. The team confirms the exact accessory list with the quote.
