# Inverters

## What we carry
Deye hybrid inverters are our core inverter line.
Single phase models (LP1 series) suit homes and small shops.
Three phase LV models (LP3 series) suit larger homes, offices, and light commercial loads.
Three phase HV models (HP3 series, 30kW to 80kW class) suit big projects running high voltage battery banks.
Exact models, sizes, current availability, and prices always come from the Warehouse Stock data, never from this file.

## Engineering rules
Inverters can only be run in parallel when they are the SAME model and size, up to 10 units per system.
Valid example: 7 x 50kW = 350kW. Invalid: mixing an 80kW with a 30kW.
A size and phase combination we do not stock is never offered; check Warehouse Stock for a row matching BOTH the size and the phase before confirming anything.

## Common questions and answers
Q: Can I mix different inverter sizes in parallel?
A: No. Parallel systems need identical models, up to 10 units together.

Q: Do I need single phase or three phase?
A: Match the building's supply. Most homes are single phase; larger buildings, offices, and industrial sites with a three phase supply need a three phase inverter.

Q: Can an LV inverter run an HV battery bank?
A: No. HV battery banks pair only with the HV three phase inverters.
