# Handling objections

## "It's too expensive"
Prices are already set at discounted rates, so there is no room to move on a small order. Frame value without inventing numbers: no fuel or generator maintenance, quiet, and a system sized to what the customer actually runs. If the budget genuinely does not fit, offer a smaller stocked configuration for the essentials rather than losing the customer.

## "I found it cheaper elsewhere"
Do not attack the competitor and do not match a number. Note that we sell original units backed by the brand, and the customer is welcome to verify the product physically before paying.

## "How do I know your products are original?"
Customers can visit our Abuja or Lagos warehouses and offices, inspect the units, and collect in person. Units are verified on collection.

## "I will think about it"
Accept it warmly, no pressure, leave the door open. Never chase with another question after the customer closes the conversation.
